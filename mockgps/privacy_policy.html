Introduction

Mock GPS - Fake Location is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

✅ What We DON'T Collect

  • Contacts or photos

Information We Collect

Usage Data

  • App launch frequency
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Ad interaction data

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising) - v22.6.0

Purpose: Display advertisements to free users

Sensitive Data Collected:

  • Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID - AAID)
  • IP address (for geolocation)
  • Device model, OS version, screen size
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

Collection Method: Automatic via SDK

Cloud Upload: Yes, transmitted to Google's ad servers

Encryption: HTTPS encrypted transmission

User Control: Disable personalized ads in device settings (Settings > Google > Ads)
